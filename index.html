Xiang Gao
xgao@ouc.edu.cn

I am a assistant professor at College of Engineering, Ocean University of China. I received my PhD from UC Berkeley, where I was advised by Jitendra Malik. I did my bachelors in ECE at NTUA in Athens, Greece, where I worked with Petros Maragos.

In the past, I have spent time at Google Brain and Google Research, where I worked with Navdeep Jaitly and Alexander Toshev.
