Xiang Gao
xgao@ouc.edu.cn

I am an assistant professor at College of Engineering, Ocean University of China since 2019.08. I received my Ph.D. degree from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I was advised by Prof. Zhanyi Hu and Prof. Shuhan Shen. Before that, I received my M.S. and B.S. degrees both from Ocean University of China, where I was advised by Prof. Zexiao Xie.

My research interest is 3D computer vision, with a particular interest in multi-source data fusion-based large-scale scene 3D reconstruction.
